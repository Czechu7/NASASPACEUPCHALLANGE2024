Introduction for players

Weather Forecast - Data that allows you to deepen your knowledge of a given scenario, analyze data and make decisions based on it.
Morale is responsible for overall satisfaction depending on the scenario of the group or person. It is influenced by things related to individuals for deterioration or improvement.
The budget represents the money resources available. It can be influenced by decisions that may cost or save money for some activity.
Insfrastructure is responsible for buildings, objects and things that are valuable, can be influenced by decisions to expand or spending an item.
Security is the level of protection of a given area or hazard scenario, it allows you to estimate how much you are at risk of a disaster, it is influenced by decisions related to the element.
